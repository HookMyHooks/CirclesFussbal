overridevirtual

Generates a medium-difficulty game map with a mix of unbreakable walls, breakable walls, and grass.

The edges of the map are filled with unbreakable walls. The inner area is randomly populated with either grass or breakable walls. Player positions are predefined at specific locations.

Parameters
player1Pointer to the first player, placed at position (1,1).
player2Pointer to the second player, placed near the bottom-right at (rows-2, columns-2).
Returns
A 2D vector of ISquare pointers representing the generated game board.
